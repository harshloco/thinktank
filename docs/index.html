Key Features

Everything you need for effective story point estimation with your team

Real-time Collaboration

Instantly see when team members join the room and cast their votes, with automatic synchronization across all devices.

Anonymous Voting

Votes remain hidden until everyone has voted or the room owner reveals them, preventing anchoring bias.

Shareable URLs

Generate unique room links that can be shared with team members for instant access to your planning session.

Dark Mode Support

Toggle between light and dark mode for comfortable estimation sessions any time of day.

Visual Results

View voting distributions and consensus stats with beautiful charts once votes are revealed.

Responsive Design

Works seamlessly on all devices, from desktops to tablets and mobile phones.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about ThinkTank Planning Poker

Do I need to create an account?

No! ThinkTank Planning Poker works without registration. Simply enter your name and you're ready to go.

Is ThinkTank Planning Poker free to use?

Yes, it's completely free and open-source. You can use it with your team without any limitations.

How long do rooms stay active?

Rooms remain active for 30 days after the last activity, giving you plenty of time for your sprint planning sessions.

Can I customize the point values?

Currently, ThinkTank Planning Poker uses the standard Fibonacci sequence (1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, etc.) for story points. Custom values are planned for future updates.
